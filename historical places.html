Historical Places in NYC

To most visitors and native New Yorkers, the city’s past is told primarily through its modern icons like the Empire State Building or through its famous museums that focus more on world history than telling the tales of New York’s past. If you are able to go beyond these well known tourist attractions, there is a wealth of historical landmarks that truly document the history of one of the oldest cities in America. Here are some of the historical landmarks in NYC:

1. Statue of Liberty

2. One World Trade Center

3. Alexander Hamilton U.S. Custom House
