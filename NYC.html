NEW YORK CITY

The City of New York is the most populous city in the United States With an estimated population of 20,537,673. A global power city,New York City has been described as the cultural, financial, and media capital of the world, and exerts a significant impact upon commerce, entertainment, research, technology, education, politics, and sports.

New York City distributed into 5 boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. Each area with its own unique charateristics.
